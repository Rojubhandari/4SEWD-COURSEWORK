Pancakes

Description

Simple and fluffy pancakes, perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Flour - 2 cups
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Eggs - 2
  • Sugar - 2 tbsp

Instructions

1. Mix all dry ingredients.
2. Add milk and eggs, whisk until smooth.
3. Cook on a hot pan until golden brown on both sides.
